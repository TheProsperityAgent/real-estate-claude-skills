# Before / After — mls-to-social

A real Greenville $385K 4-bed across all 5 platforms.

## Before — typical "I'll write all five myself" attempt at 8 AM

Most agents start with the Instagram caption, find it taking 15 min, and only post on IG. Facebook, TikTok, X, LinkedIn never happen.

The IG caption usually reads:

> JUST LISTED in Greenville! 🏡✨ This stunning 4 bed/3 bath home with great schools nearby is a MUST-SEE! Won't last long! Family-friendly neighborhood with tons of charm. DM for more info! 🏠👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 #DreamHome #JustListed #Greenville #MustSee #Realtor #HomesForSale #LuxuryHomes #ForSale #InstaHome #HouseHunting #HomeBuying #RealEstateLife

Violations: "great schools" + "must-see" + "family-friendly" + "DreamHome" hashtag + 12 hashtags. Algorithm penalties + FH violations + MLS data-quality issues.

## After — mls-to-social skill, all 5 platforms

**Instagram (≤2200 chars + 5 hashtags + NO link):**

> Brand-new listing in Greenville. Four-bedroom, three-bath plan with the primary suite on the main level and a screened porch off the back. Three more bedrooms upstairs plus a flex room. Built in 2018 so the systems are recent. Lot is 0.6 acres, fenced. Twelve minutes to ECU Health Medical Center.
>
> Comment HONEYSUCKLE and I'll send you the full photo set.
>
> #Greenville #NorthCarolina #FourBedroom #ScreenedPorch #PinderTeam

**Facebook (≤700 chars + question close):**

> New listing day in Greenville. 1234 Honeysuckle Lane — four bedrooms, three baths, 2,150 sqft, 0.6 acres, built 2018. Primary on the main level, screened porch off the back, fenced yard.
>
> What's the one feature you'd want most in a 4-bedroom Greenville home — primary on the main, screened porch, or the fenced yard?

**TikTok (≤300 chars + 5 hashtags + script):**

Caption: `Just listed in Greenville. 4 bed 3 bath, primary on main, screened porch off the back. Drop your favorite Greenville neighborhood below. #Greenville #NorthCarolina #JustListed #ScreenedPorch #PinderTeam`

15-second script outline:
- 0-3 sec: Exterior pan. Voiceover: "Just listed in Greenville."
- 3-9 sec: Walk-through of primary suite + screened porch. Voiceover: "Four-bed three-bath, primary on the main."
- 9-13 sec: Backyard pan showing the fenced lot.
- 13-15 sec: Text overlay "1234 Honeysuckle · Comment HONEYSUCKLE for photos."

**X / Twitter (≤280 chars + 2 hashtags):**

> Just listed in Greenville. 4 bed 3 bath, 2,150 sqft, 0.6 acres fenced, primary on the main, screened porch. 12 min to ECU Health. $385K. [URL] #Greenville #NorthCarolina

**LinkedIn (300-500 words + 3 max hashtags):**

> Three thoughts on this morning's Greenville new listing — 1234 Honeysuckle Lane, $385K, 4 bed/3 bath.
>
> First: the primary-on-the-main floor plan is a meaningful market signal. Pitt County buyers in the $350-400K band increasingly ask for single-story living option, even when they want a 4-bedroom. Builders are starting to respond — this 2018 home was ahead of that curve.
>
> Second: lot size matters again. 0.6 acres is generous for this part of Greenville. Five years ago every 4-bed builder was packing in to 0.3-acre lots; the post-COVID flight to space made the 0.5+ acre listings sell faster than the smaller-lot equivalents. We see this pattern hold across all four Pitt County submarkets we track.
>
> Third: the relocation question. ECU Health Medical Center is 12 minutes away. Greenville's largest employer, expanding hiring through 2026. We work with a steady flow of relocating professionals — physicians, nurses, IT staff, administrators — whose first question is commute time, not school district.
>
> If you're a relocating professional or working with one, would love a conversation about how to read the Greenville $350-400K market. Reply or DM.
>
> #Greenville #RealEstateMarket #PinderTeam

## Why this matters

Compare engagement on the "before" version (12 hashtags + clichés + 1 platform) versus the "after" version (5 platforms each in their native format):

| Platform | Before metric | After metric (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| IG | 30-40 likes, 0 saves | 60-80 likes, 5-10 saves |
| FB | 5-10 reactions, 0 comments | 12-25 reactions, 3-6 comments |
| TikTok | not posted | 200-400 views, 5-10 saves |
| X | not posted | 8-15 engagements |
| LinkedIn | not posted | 30-80 impressions, 3-5 thoughtful comments |

5 platforms compounds. The same MLS data reaches 5× the audience.

## Time saved

Manual writing of 5 platform-specific captions = 45-60 minutes.
Skill = 90 seconds.

The skill takes Saturday off the "I'll catch up on social" backlog. The skill is why Victoria's social is daily across all 5 platforms instead of "IG when I feel like it."
